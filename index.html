Overview

Established in 2016, Crofarm is a pioneer agri-tech startup focused on building farmer's communities and helping them serve fresh, safe and nutritious food to consumers. With a team of the best Technology and Agri-enthusiasts, we are solving the problem of fresh produce wastage by building a fast and sustainable supply chain that rewards farmers for growing better food

Purpose & Value

Crofarm is on a mission to bring farmers and consumers together on a fair platform where consumers have access to fresh, nutritious and affordable produce.
